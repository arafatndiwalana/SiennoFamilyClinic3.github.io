FAQs

Home / FAQs

What services do you offer?

We provide a comprehensive range of services, including primary care, Laboratory Services, emergency services, Antenatal And Postnatal Services, maternity care,Public Health And Family Planning. Our focus is on delivering top-notch healthcare tailored to the needs of our community.

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment in several ways to ensure it’s convenient for you. Firstly, you can call our main line and speak directly with our friendly staff, who will assist you in finding a suitable time with the right specialist. Our lines are open during business hours, but you can leave a voicemail after hours, and we will return your call promptly.

Additionally, you can visit our website and use the online appointment scheduling tool, which allows you to select your preferred date, time, and doctor. For those who prefer managing everything digitally, our patient portal offers the convenience of booking appointments, viewing your medical history, and even receiving reminders. We strive to make your scheduling experience as seamless as possible.

Do you have specialists available?

Yes, we have a wide range of specialists available, including cardiologists, neurologists, orthopedists, and more. You can find a full list of our specialists on our website.

How can I contact you?

You can contact us by phone, email, or through our website’s contact form. Our contact information is readily available on our website for your convenience.

What is your billing process?

After receiving care, you will receive a detailed bill outlining the services provided. Our billing department is available to answer any questions and assist with payment plans if needed. You can also pay your bill online through our patient portal.
